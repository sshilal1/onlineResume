My Projects


Just some of things I have worked on in my spare time.

Neighborhood Map

An interactive map of Anchorage, Alaska - Interfacing with the Google Maps and Yelp API's

Completed as part of the Udacity Front-End Nanodegree program, I used Ajax to make requests to the 2 APIS, and then display the information cleanly and in order with jQuery, and Knockout.

This was also my first endeavor using Github, which I have come to love and use every day.

Solar Array Dashboard

A dashboard displaying information for the solar panels on the roof of the Engineering and Science Building at Binghamton University

As team lead of our Sr. Design Project at my University, I was tasked with managing the creation and implementation of this dashboard. Using Javascript Photoshop, and Bootstrap, our team created the interactive display

This was my introduction to web development, and I knew right away it was something I wanted to do
